Recommendations

Do's: Positive Steps to Manage Osteoporosis

  • Maintain a Balanced Diet Rich in Calcium and Vitamin
  • Engage in Regular Physical Activity
  • Undergo Regular Bone Density Tests
  • Take Prescribed Medications
  • Quit Smoking and Limit Alcohol Consumption
  • Ensure Safe Living Environments
  • Consult with a Physical Therapist
  • Stay Informed

Don'ts: Things to Avoid

  • Avoid High-Impact Exercises
  • Limit Intake of Caffeine and High-Salt Foods
  • Do not Skip Medication Doses
  • Limit Alcohol Intake
  • Avoid Situations Where Falls Are Likely
  • Avoid Smoking
  • Be Cautious with Over-the-Counter Supplements
